## What You'll Build

A complete microservices architecture with:
- Multiple services with clear boundaries
- Synchronous (REST/gRPC) and async (Kafka) communication
- Distributed tracing, metrics, and logging
- Docker Compose and Kubernetes deployment
- Circuit breakers, retries, and bulkheads
